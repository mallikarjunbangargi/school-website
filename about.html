About us

Mandara pushpa school started in 2005 under the aegis of Satlingappa Metre, the school is located 300 mtr From Kalaburgi aland road Check post, surrounded by very peaceful RAMTEERTH TEMPLE. The school caters to the students from nearby area & all over the Kalaburgi City. Students who come from the background of illiterate & comprise of farm laborer quarry workers. Majority of the students are first generation learners.

Kannada is a Medium of instruction. Students are also exposed to computers & English at a very Early Age. A National language Hindi is taught from class 5th std onwards.

The school lays Emphasis on a set of “Core values” which are inculcated & fostered in a supportive Environment that respect the individual & the diversities they bring in. The school provides inputs to students to focus on their physical, Emotional, Cultural & Moral Development apart from their studies.

A great person Swami Vivekananda’s Dream of Education through “Man making & Character Building” Where Assimilation of ideas is important & actualizing the potentials of every child is attempted here in the school. The school aims to produce morally upright emotionally stable, self reliant, self confident & intelligent individuals of this land. The school follows an enriched Curriculum for Mathematics & science (Abacus & Vedic) Drawing from international curricula.
